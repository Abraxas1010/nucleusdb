# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to a respectful, harassment-free collaboration environment for all contributors.

## Expected Behavior

- be respectful and constructive
- assume good intent and discuss disagreements technically
- focus critiques on code and decisions, not people
- help maintain a professional and inclusive project space

## Unacceptable Behavior

- harassment, abuse, or intimidation
- discriminatory or hateful language
- personal attacks or doxxing
- deliberate disruption of project workflows

## Enforcement

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing this policy and may remove or edit contributions that violate it.

## Reporting

Report conduct issues privately to:

- `conduct@apoth3osis.org`

All reports will be reviewed confidentially.
